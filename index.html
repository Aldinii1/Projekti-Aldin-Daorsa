Hey there, and welcome to A&D!

Founded in 2024 by Daorsa and Aldin, A&D has quickly become the go-to spot for amazing fast food in the heart of Prishtina. Were all about serving delicious, fresh, and satisfying meals that keep you coming back for more. In just a short time, weve grown to six awesome locations across Prishtina, including: Bregu i Diellit, Lakrishte, Ulpiana, Dardania, Arbëria, and Mati 1. No matter where you are in the city, youre never too far from your favorite A&D flavors! Whether youre craving a quick snack, a hearty meal, or just a friendly place to grab a bite, were here to make it happen. Thanks for being part of our journey we cant wait to serve you at A&D!
